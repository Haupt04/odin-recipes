Amasi Scones

Description

Ingredients

  • 500g Margarine
  • 4 eggs
  • 30ml baking powder
  • 250ml sugar

Steps

  1. Sift flour, baking powder, sugar and salt into large metal bowl and mix around gently with a spatula.
  2. Rub in margarine until mixture resembles fine breadcrumbs and make a well in the center of the bowl.
  3. Whisk together egg, amasi and milk in a separate bowl and pour 2/3 of this into the middle of the crumb mixture.
